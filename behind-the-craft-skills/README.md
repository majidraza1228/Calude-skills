# Behind the Craft — AI skills

11 hand-crafted skills for Claude Code or Codex.

## How to install

1. Open Claude Code or Codex in your project.
2. Tell it: "Install these skills" and reference this folder (or drag it in).
3. Each subfolder has a `SKILL.md` and any extras you should customize first (e.g., `plan.md`).
4. Skills install to `~/.claude/skills/` (global) or `./.claude/skills/` (project-local).

## Skills

### Personal

- `personal-advisor/`: **Personal advisor**. My favorite skill. Get honest advice grounded in your goals, energy, and life situation.
- `health-coach/`: **Health coach**. Get evidence-based tips on workouts, meals, and sleep grounded in your goals and routines.
- `weekend-planner/`: **Weekend planner**. Find 10 time-sensitive things to do personalized for you and your family for an upcoming weekend.

### Product

- `strategy-writer/`: **Strategy writer**. Shape a one-page product strategy with vision, principles, and milestones.
- `spec-writer/`: **Spec writer**. Turn your rough notes into a clear PRD with my personal template.
- `exec-reviewer/`: **Exec reviewer**. Stress-test a plan, strategy, or doc before the real review. It ships with a strong built-in reviewer, so it works with zero setup, and can learn your specific leader's patterns if you want.

### Creator

- `social-writer/`: **Social writer**. Turn a draft into 3 LinkedIn/X posts based on my most viral examples.
- `infographic-designer/`: **Infographic designer**. Design a branded 9:16 infographic from an idea, ready to publish on social.
- `no-ai-slop/`: **No AI slop**. Removes all the AI slop from any writing excerpt.

### Builder

- `skill-editor/`: **Skill editor**. Audit and tighten an existing AI skill, cut AI-speak and bloat, then eval it before you ship.
- `slides/`: **Slide builder**. Build a polished HTML slide deck with Peter's Paper / Ink / Red brand style, 12 canonical slide formats, animated SVG illustrations, and a built-in Visual QA pass.
