## Strategy
<!-- The guiding policy: how we'll win. 2 short paragraphs -->

[Example: We win by going where the search query already lives. Knowledge workers already type questions into Slack and the browser address bar dozens of times a day. We piggyback on that habit with a Chrome extension, a Slack bot, and a desktop shortcut that intercept the moment of confusion and answer inline with citations from the user's existing tools. Competitors build destination apps; we build a layer that disappears.

We win because we're the only player with both the federation infrastructure (connectors for Slack, Notion, Drive, Linear) and the patience to start invisible. Incumbents like Glean and Microsoft Copilot chase the destination-app market because that's where the contracts are. We trade short-term ARPU for deeper habit lock-in and the long bet that ambient beats destination.]
